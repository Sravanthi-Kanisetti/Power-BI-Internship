📊 Job Market Analysis Dashboard
This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze job market trends, skill demand, and salary insights. It transforms raw job data into actionable career decisions for job seekers.
🔍 Objective
To understand:
Which industries are hiring the most
Which skills are in high demand
How salary varies by role, industry, and experience
What career strategies can be derived from data
📁 Dashboard Structure
1. Job Market Trends Analysis
Industry-wise job demand
City-wise hiring distribution
Salary growth by experience
Company-wise salary comparison
2. Skill Demand & Career Analysis
Top 5 in-demand skills based on ~2.6K–2.7K job demand each
Skill demand vs salary relationship
Skill distribution across jobs
Identification of high-paying skills based on average salary (LPA)
3. Salary Trends & Growth Analysis
Salary comparison across job roles
Industry-wise salary distribution
Job distribution by salary range
Demand vs salary correlation
4. Career Strategy & Key Takeaways
Key insights derived from data
Step-by-step career growth strategy
Actionable recommendations for job seekers
💡 Key Insights
Analyzed 10K+ job listings across multiple industries and roles
AI/ML industry has the highest hiring demand
SQL is the most in-demand skill across jobs
Most job opportunities fall in the 10–20 LPA range
High demand roles do not always offer the highest salary
Salary differences across roles are minimal (~14.9–15.2 LPA range) but consistent
🎯 Career Recommendations
Start with SQL-based Data Analyst roles
Add Power BI to increase salary potential
Gain 2–5 years experience for career growth
Transition to AI/ML roles for higher salaries
Target high-paying industries like FinTech

⭐ Final Note
This dashboard provides a complete view of the job market by combining demand, salary trends, and skill analysis, helping users make informed career decisions.
